Compliance & Transparency

🌍 Global Privacy Compliance

EthervoxAI is designed to comply with privacy regulations worldwide:

  • EU GDPR: Full compliance with European data protection standards
  • UK GDPR: Compliant with UK data protection requirements
  • California CCPA/CPRA: Including "Do Not Sell" rights
  • US State Laws: Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah privacy acts
  • Google Play Policies: Adherence to all Play Store requirements

🔐 Security Measures

We implement industry-leading security practices:

  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: Military-grade encryption for all stored data
  • Hardware-Backed Keys: Android Keystore with TEE protection
  • TLS 1.2+: Encrypted network communications
  • App-Private Storage: Data isolated from other applications
  • No Cloud Backup: Prevents unencrypted backups

🎯 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have specific privacy rights:

  • Access: View all data we hold about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Export: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Opt-Out: Decline personalized advertising (upgrade to Premium)
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Lodge Complaint: File complaints with supervisory authorities

Contact us to exercise your rights.

Categories of Personal Information

We collect the following categories (through AdMob in free tier only):

  • Identifiers (Advertising ID, device ID)
  • Internet activity (ad interactions)
  • Geolocation data (approximate, country-level)
  • Commercial information (purchase history for subscriptions)
  • Device information (OS version, model)

Do Not Sell My Personal Information

AdMob data sharing may constitute a "sale" under California law. You can opt-out:

  • In the app: Settings → Privacy → Do Not Sell My Personal Information
  • Or upgrade to Premium to eliminate all ad-related data collection

Non-Discrimination

We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights. However, the free tier requires ad consent as it's our only means of supporting free users.
